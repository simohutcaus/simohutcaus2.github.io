Reckon One is a cloud accounting system for small business with the ability to add and remove modules as the business needs change.

CashBooks ¶

Resources related to CashBooks in the Reckon One API.

CashBooks collection  ¶

List all CashBooks
GET/cashbooks

This action is used to retrieve all CashBooks the user has access to.

  • BookName (String) - The name of the book

  • BookStatus - The status of the book (0 -Active, 1- Inactive, 3 - Deactivated)

Post a single bank account
POST/bankaccounts/

  • AccountType (Number) - The type of account, cash or credit (1 - Cash, 2 - Credit)

  • BankAccountStatus (Number) - Whether the bank account is open or closed (1 - Open, 2 - Closed)

  • BulkElectronicPaymentSettings - Not used in API

  • IncludeBalancingTransaction (Boolean) - Whether to include a balance transaction for the opening of the account

  • PayerIdentifier (Alphanumeric) - The APCA number for the bank account

  • DateFrom (Date/Time) - The opening date of the account in universal time

  • Description (String) - The description of the bank account

  • EFT (String) - The details of the bank account for electronic payments such as BSB/Account details

  • EFT1 (Numeric) - The BSB of the account

  • EFT2 (Numeric) - The account number of the bank accuont

  • IsCash - Not used in the API

  • LockOffDate (Date/Time) - The account lock off time in universal time

Contacts ¶

Resources related to Contacts in the Reckon One API.

Contacts Collection  ¶

  • ContactID (GUID) - The GUID of the contact

  • CashBookID - The CashBook the contact is from

  • PaymentTermID (GUID) - The payment term that is set against the contact in the Reckon One book

  • CustomerCreditLimit (Number) - Credit limit set against the contact in the Reckon One book

  • IsTpar (Boolean) - Whether the contact is set to appear in the TPAR report or not

  • Description (String) - Description for the contact the user has entered into the Reckon One book

  • FirstNameBranchName (String) - If personal: the first name of the contact, if business: the branch name/number of the business

  • SurnameBusinessName (String) - If personal: the last name of the contact, if business: the business name

  • IsCustomer (Boolean) - Whether the contact is set to a customer type

  • IsSupplier (Boolean) - Whether the contact is set to a supplier type

  • IsActive (Boolean) - Whether the contact is set to active

  • IsPerson (Boolean) - Whether the contact is set as a personal or business contact. True indicates personal, False indicates business

  • MobileCode (Number) - The prefix of the mobile number for the region. In Australia this is typically 04 and in New Zealand 021

  • MobileNumber (Nubmer) - The number of the mobile minus the prefix

  • Email (String) - The email of the contact

  • Website (String) - The website of the contact

  • BusinessNumber1 - The tax registration id of the contact. In Australia this is ABN, In New Zealand it is the IRD number

  • Notes (String) - Notes against the contact

  • AreaCode (Number) - The area code for the phone number of the contact

  • PhoneNumber (Number) - The phone number minus the area code of the contact

  • FaxCode (Number) - The area code for the fax number of the contact

  • FaxNumber (Number) - The fax number of the contact minus the area code

  • IsBusinessAddressDifferent (Boolean) - Indicates whether there is multiple addresses set for the contact

  • IsEmployee (Boolean) - Indicates whether the contact is set to employee

  • Address Array (Array) - Each address contained in an array

  • AddressID (GUID) - The unique id stored in the Reckon One book to identify the address

  • Line1 (String) - Line 1 of the address of the contact

  • Line2 (String) - Line 2 of the address of the contact

  • Line3 (String) - Line 3 of the address of the contact

  • Town (String) - The town of the contact (International Addresses only)

  • Suburb (String) - The suburb of the contact (Domestic Addresses only)

  • State (String) - The state of the contact

  • Postcode (String) - The postcode of the contact. Has validation for Australian addresses of 4 numeric numbers

  • Country (String) - The country of the contact (International Addresses only)

  • AddressType (Boolean) - Type of the address set in the contact

  • IsInternational (Boolean) - Whether this is a international address or not

Items ¶

Resources related to Items in the Reckon One API.

Items Collection  ¶

  • Id (GUID) - The GUID of the item

  • Name/FullName (String) - Name of the item (Name on POST/FullName on GET)

  • SalePrice Number (Number) - The sale price of the item

  • SalePriceTaxInclusive - No longer used in API

  • SaleTaxId (GUID) - The GUID of the tax code associated with the item

  • SaleDescription (String) - The sales description of the item

  • PurchasePrice (Number) - The purchase price of the item

  • PurchaseTaxId (GUID) - The GUID of the tax code associated with the item

  • PurchaseDescription (String) - The purchase description of the item

  • IsActive (Boolean) - Whether the item is active or not

  • IsPurchase (Boolean) - Whether the item is purchased or not

  • IsSold (Boolean) - Whether the item is sold or not

  • ItemCode (String) - The item code of the item

  • ProjectRateApplied (Boolean) - Whether a project rate should be applied to this item

  • SoldOrPurchased (Number) - Whether the item is sold and purchased by the business (0 - Sell only, 1 - Buy Only, 2 - Buy and Sell)

  • ItemStatus (Number) - Whether the item is active in the book or not (0 - Active, 1 - InActive)

  • SaleCategoryID (GUID) - The GUID of the account to be associated with the sales item

  • PurchaseCategoryID (GUID) - The GUID of the account to be associated with the purchase item

  • IsTaxInclusive (Boolean) - Whether the sale and purchase amounts include tax

  • ParentChargeableItemID (GUID) - If the item is a subitem, the GUID of the parent item

  • ItemType (Number) - The type of the item be it a product or service (1 - Product, 2 - Service)

Journals ¶

Resources related to journals in the Reckon One API.

Journals Collection  ¶

  • Adjusting Journal (Boolean) - Whether the journal is adjusting or not

  • AmountTaxStatus (Number) - Whether the amounts on the journal include tax or not (0 - Not utilised, 1 - Non-Taxed, 2 - Inclusive, 3 - Exclusive)

  • CashBookID (GUID) - The GUID of the Reckon One book the journal is from

  • FullDescription (String) - The description of the journal

  • JournalDate (Date/Time) - The date of the journal

  • JournalID (GUID) - The GUID of the journal

  • JournalNumber (Number) - The number of the journal

  • JournalStatus (Number) - The status of the journal (0 - Unknown 1 - Draft, 2 - Posted, 3 - Reversed, 4 - All)

  • JournalType (Number) - The type of the journal (0 - General, 1 - Bank Opening Balance, 2 - Money Transfers)

  • ReverseJournalID (GUID) - The GUID of the reverse journal

  • Summary (String) - The summary of the journal entered by the user

  • Transactions Array (Array) - The individual transactions of the journal

  • AccountingCategoryID (GUID) - The id of the account being used on the line

  • Credit (Number) - The amount being credited on that line

  • Debit (Number) - The amount beign debited on that line

  • IsReconciled (Boolean) - Whether the transaction has been reconciled by the user

  • JournalEntryId (GUID) - The unique GUID assigned to that entry on the journal

  • Narration (String) - The narration entered for that line transaction on the journal

  • ProjectID (GUID) - The GUID of the project assigned to the entry on the journal

  • TaxAmount (Number) - The amount of tax assigned to that line in the journal

  • TaxGroupID (GUID) - The unique id of the tax code assigned to that line in the journal

  • TaxModified (Boolean) - Whether the tax has been modified from the books calculation

  • TransType (Number) - The type of transaction that the journal line entry is (0 - Not set, 1 - Sale, 2 - Purchase, 3 - Transfer)
